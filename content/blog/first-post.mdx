## The Rise of a New Industry

Chile is quickly becoming a hub for biotechnology in Latin America. With its unique biodiversity, strong academic research, and growing government support, the country is poised for significant growth in this sector.

### Key Growth Areas

*   **Aquaculture:** Improving salmon farming through genetic research.
*   **Agriculture:** Developing drought-resistant crops.
*   **Health:** New diagnostics and therapies for prevalent diseases.

The future is bright for biotech in Chile, and we are excited to be a part of it.
